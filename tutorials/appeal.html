How to appeal a ban or mute on Minehut

Information before continuing

If you have already contacted support, or if they tell you your ban or mute is valid then there is no point in reading this tutorial



So, let's learn how to appeal!

Minehut has it's very own form to submit an appeal, which you can use to appeal
NOTE: You may need to log in or create a Minehut account to appeal!



Step 1

Go to the form that Minehut has provided for you here
Fill out all the information required so they can contact you and possibly find the subjected punishment to locate the cause

Step 2

Minehut Support usually takes 1-3 days to reply to you. You should get an email on the address you signed up to Minehut with.

They will reply to you with the information you need, querying your punishment.
